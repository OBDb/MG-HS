# MG HS

This repository contains signal set configurations for the MG HS, organized by model year and version. The files are structured to allow for easy differentiation between model generations and other vehicle parameters, ensuring accurate signal mapping for each version of the MG HS.

## Generations

The MG HS is a compact crossover SUV that has established itself as a value-oriented alternative in the competitive SUV segment, evolving through two distinct generations:

- **First Generation (2018-2024)**: Launched as a replacement for the MG GS, the HS debuted at the 2018 Beijing Auto Show as the production version of the MG X-Motion concept. Key characteristics included:
  - Built on SAIC's modular platform shared with Roewe RX5
  - 1.5L inline-four turbo petrol engine (169 hp / 126 kW)
  - 2.0L inline-four turbo petrol engine (231 hp / 172 kW)
  - Standard dual-clutch transmission (DCT)
  - MG Pilot safety suite with emergency braking and lane assist
  - Traffic jam assist functionality up to 56 km/h (35 mph)

  **Global Market Expansion:**
  - 2019: Initial European markets launch
  - 2020: Indonesia launch (August 13) with Excite and Ignite trims
  - 2020: Singapore debut at Motor Show (January 9)
  - 2022: Philippines launch (March 17) with Alpha and Trophy trims
  - 2023: Indonesia facelift (January 25)

- **Second Generation (2024-present)**: Completely redesigned model unveiled at the 2024 Goodwood Festival of Speed (July 11), based on the Chinese market second-generation Roewe RX5. Major improvements include:
  - Enhanced design language with modern styling
  - Expanded powertrain options including electrification
  - 1.5L turbo petrol engine (base configuration)
  - Plug-in Hybrid (PHEV) variant with 113 km electric range and 303 hp total output
  - 2025 Hybrid+ model with 224 PS (165 kW) and 340 Nm torque
  - Improved performance: 0-62 mph in 6.8 seconds (PHEV), 102 mph top speed

  **Recent Global Launches:**
  - September 2024: Australia (Vibe, Excite, Essence trims)
  - November 2024: Malaysia (Standard and Lux variants)
  - December 2024: South Africa market return (Comfort and Luxury trims)

  **Technical Specifications (Second Generation):**
  - Advanced infotainment and connectivity features
  - Enhanced safety systems and driver assistance
  - Improved interior space and build quality
  - Seven-year, 80,000-mile warranty coverage
  - Positioning above the subcompact MG ZS in global lineup
